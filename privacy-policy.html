Privacy Policy

Effective Date

This Privacy Policy is effective as of April 20, 2025.

1. Introduction

tye ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our family event management application (the "App").

2. Information We Collect

2.1. Personal Information

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, password, and family details (e.g., names and ages of family members).

Profile Information: You can create profiles for parents and children, which may include names, photos, birthdays, and other relevant details.

Event and Activity Data: We collect data related to events, holidays, activities, to-do lists, and grocery lists that you create and manage within the App.

Contact Information: If you choose to import contacts, we may collect names and contact details from your address book.

Communications: We collect information when you contact us for support or feedback.

2.2. Usage Information

Log Data: We collect log data about your use of the App, including your IP address, device information, browser type, and pages visited.

Analytics Data: We use analytics tools to collect data about how you use the App, such as features used, time spent, and interactions.

3. How We Use Your Information

Provide and Improve the App: We use your information to operate, maintain, and improve the App's functionality and user experience.

Personalization: We use your data to personalize the App, such as suggesting relevant events, activities, and reminders.

Communications: We send you notifications, updates, and support messages related to the App.

Analytics: We analyze usage data to understand how users interact with the App and to improve our services.

Legal Compliance: We may use your information to comply with legal obligations, such as responding to legal requests.

4. How We Share Your Information

Within Your Family: Information you share within the App is visible to other family members in your account.

Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who assist us with hosting, analytics, customer support, and other services. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information.

Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or legal process.

Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is designed for family use, which may include children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information without your consent, please contact us, and we will take steps to delete the information. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar laws globally.

7. International Data Transfers

As we operate globally, your information may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your country of residence, which may have different data protection laws. We take steps to ensure your data is protected in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

8. Your Rights

Access and Correction: You have the right to access and correct your personal information.

Deletion: You can request the deletion of your account and personal information.

Opt-Out: You can opt-out of receiving promotional communications.

Data Portability: You can request a copy of your data in a portable format.

9. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide the App and fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on our website or through the App.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at help@gettye.com.
